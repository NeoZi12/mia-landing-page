Follow instructions exactly.

Rules:

- do not change layout, structure, styling, or content unless explicitly asked
- do not make extra improvements outside the request
- do not rewrite unrelated parts
- preserve responsiveness unless explicitly asked to change it
- keep edits minimal and precise

Viewport handling:

- when working on full sections or pages, ensure they properly fit the viewport height (100vh) when appropriate
- do not force 100vh on elements where it would break layout or content flow
- maintain clean and correct full-screen sections without causing overflow issues
